GAPSA LIAISON

  • GAPSA Liaison represents Rangoli at the GASPA Select Committee.
  • The duties involve working to represent Rangoli to GAPSA, advertise the group’s events/activities to the Penn community through the GAPSA channel, and help increase the presence of Rangoli in the Penn community by stepping up on opportunities through GAPSA in campus-wide drives.
  • He/she is required to attend Rangoli Board meetings, help design budget plans, attend GAPSA Select Committee meetings, send monthly updates to GAPSA correspondent about Rangoli’s activities, attend GAPSA general assembly bimonthly meetings, and ensure smooth functioning of the group procedures in accordance with constitutional protocols.
